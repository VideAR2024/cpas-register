Why Sign Up?

Because right now, nobody can see you

The moment you leave shore or launch into the air, you vanish from every tracking system. If something goes wrong, rescue crews are searching blind. CPAS changes that.

Who Can Join?

If you go where rescue services can't see you

The pilot is open to anyone who uses the South Coast's waters or low-level airspace — whether you're part of a club or paddle out solo.

🚣 Kayaking 🏄 SUP / Paddle Boarding ⛵ Sailing 🏊 Open Water Swimming 🎣 Fishing 🪂 Paragliding 🛩️ Paramotor Flying

Not in a club? No problem. Individual participants are just as welcome. You don't need to be a member of anything — you just need to be someone who goes out there.
